About Us

We are a family-owned business located in Hilton, New York. Owner is Frank Palumbo. Buttonwood Farms has been around since 1989. We have plants, trees, mulch, and stone. We open in April and close late November. We do everything from deliveries to ordeing plants and trees for the customer. We hope that with our assistance, we can help you with all of your plant and tree needs. Our goal is to improve the community one tree at a time.
